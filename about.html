About Current Project

I have always been interested in the world of creating content ever since I was young, making videos about games.

I have recently been strongly desiring to start a business, also because of the freedom and independence it gives me.

I did roughly planned out what to include as my product.

And I learnt that Businesses get customers through either:

  • Warm and Cold Outreach
  • Paid Ads
  • Content
Realised I could do both Content and Business at the same time, why not do that for my guided learning, for extra accountability?

My Planned Business

After they enjoy learning my stuff and trust me, they could be led to my free community, where I:
