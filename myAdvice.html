For people who (probably) want to take Guided Learning:

  1. Don't choose Guided Learning solely because you want to avoid doing the other module.
  2. Choose a project that you will truly enjoy and have passion for.
  3. You need to learn to be independent, nobody is going to give you the deadlines (except the last interview).
  4. You do not have to strictly follow the plan you laid out, things can and will change.
  5. Enjoy! It's the only subject where you can be completely indepedent.

How I managed my time

From Ali Abdaal, I learnt that the content creation process can be split into 6 parts:

  • Ideation
  • Thumbnail and Title
  • Scripting
  • Filming
  • Editing
  • Upload

So ideally, I will just split those 6 process throughout the week.

  • Monday: Ideation
  • Tuesday: Title and Thumbnail
  • Wednesday: Writing
  • Thursday: Filming and Editing
  • Friday: Editing
  • Saturday: Upload
  • Sunday: Rest

But, during school week, I could split them easily. So what I do is:

  • Monday: -
  • Tuesday: -
  • Wednesday: Ideation and Scripting
  • Thursday (Free the whole day): Thumbnail, Title, Filming, Editing
  • Friday: Editing
  • Saturday: Upload
  • Sunday: Rest

I like this idea by an entrepreneur, Alex Hormozi:

The best time to to start any venture is when you are busy. Because when you make it work when you are busy, you will make it work when you are busy again.

Furthermore, you will more time will you are free.
